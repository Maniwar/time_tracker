About

Universal Time Tracker with Deliverables & Goals
Version 2.3.0

This extension helps you track your time accurately across Google Calendar and Outlook, including when you're multi-tasking during meetings. Now with deliverables and goals tracking to help you focus on what matters most.

New Features:
• Goals & Impact tracking - Define what you want to achieve
• Deliverables management - Track work towards specific outcomes
• Progress visualization - See how you're doing against goals
• Google Calendar integration with one-click setup
• Universal calendar view for both Google and Outlook
• Meeting mode with multi-tasking support
• Auto-tracking for scheduled meetings
• Dual timer display for simultaneous tracking
• Multi-tasking analysis in exports
• Productivity scoring based on focus time

Privacy: All data is stored locally in your browser. No information is sent to external servers except for calendar sync with your authenticated accounts.
